OVERVIEW OF THE VIETNAMESE ALPHABET

Each language has its own alphabet and each alphabet has its own unique features to create its own characteristics

So do you wonder what the Vietnamese alphabet will be like?

From:UK Academy

The Vietnamese alphabet is currently used based on the Latin alphabet. This alphabet has some characteristics similar to the English alphabet. The following will be the Vietnamese alphabet

As you can see, it is similar to the English alphabet in terms of letter order. The Vietnamese alphabet has up to 29 characters.

However, the Vietnamese alphabet has some special features:

+ 1. Some letters have been added with accents to form another letter For example: the letter A when added with accents will form another letter. We have two letters that are variations of the letter A, which are "Ă" and "Â"

+ 2. In the Vietnamese alphabet, we do not use letters like F, J, W, Z.

Conclusion: That is a brief overview of the Vietnamese alphabet.
